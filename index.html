About Virgil

Virgil van Dijk, born on July 8, 1991, in Breda, Netherlands, is a world-class centre-back who captains both Liverpool FC and the Netherlands national team. Known for his strength, leadership, and composure, he is widely regarded as one of the best defenders of his generation.

Career Highlights

  • Started professional career at FC Groningen
  • Won Scottish Premiership with Celtic
  • Joined Liverpool in 2018 for a record fee for a defender
  • Won the UEFA Champions League in 2019
  • Helped Liverpool win the Premier League in 2020
  • Named UEFA Men's Player of the Year in 2019
